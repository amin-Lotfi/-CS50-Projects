Nostalgia

I'm fascinated by music genres that evoke nostalgia and surreal reinterpretations of the past. Albums like 'News at 11' by the rather unknown Dutch producer Jornt Elzinga/Cat System Corp. captivate me with their unique blend of smooth jazz and ambient sounds, creating a parallel universe where time stands still. Other random artists also transport me to a time that feels both familiar and unexplored. Their music, sometimes a dreamy and melancholic rendition of the 80s and other times an adult dive into peaceful jazz, truly represents a journey through sound that resonates with my soul. (And I think I want to take a walk through the city after writing this.)
