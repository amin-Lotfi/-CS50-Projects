1707

It's strange how certain moments stick with you.

I was only 11 when JJ3054 crashed near where I lived. I remember the events unfolding on TV.

At the time, I was too young and stupid to understand the gravity of the tragedy. All I could think about was the fact that Air Crash Investigation was such a cool show and maybe they would make an episode about it.

This is far from being any fun, however. It's a homage to those lost in these tragedies and an opportunity to learn from them. Flying has never been safer, and it's thanks to the hard work of the people who investigate incidents and accidents.
